NSE V/S BSE

What is NSE?

NSE or National Stock Exchange was founded in 1992 and is comparatively the youngest stock exchange in India. But, it got its recognition as a stock exchange only in 1993. They pioneered the trading industry by introducing fully automated, electronic, and screen-based trading in India. They have provided a secure platform for trading shares and bonds, electronically, since 1995.The electronic trading system helped remove the paper-based settlement system from trading.

What is BSE?

Although there are only a few differences between NSE and BSE, we have to be very sure of which one to choose, especially if you are new to investing. If you are a beginner and new to this field, it is always better to invest in BSE, whereas NSE is for seasoned investors. In case you are looking for a new company to invest in then, BSE is the best option. On the other hand, if you are a day trader or likes to take risks then NSE is more suitable for you.
